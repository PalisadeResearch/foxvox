FoxVox is an open-source Chrome extension powered by GPT-4. We created FoxVox to demonstrate how AI can be used to subtly manipulate the content you consume.

Why is this dangerous?

  • Siloed realities: Companies, governments, and cyber criminals already know a lot about you. Cookies track your internet activity, and companies pay data brokers hundreds of billions of dollars to influence which content lands in your newsfeed. But now, news providers, marketers, and political campaigns could rewrite articles on the fly, tailored to your medical condition, political beliefs, or the age of your children. You’d never even know that the content had been optimized to conform to your worldview.
  • Propaganda: Effective propaganda often involves interpreting true facts to favor a specific viewpoint, as opposed to spreading outright lies. AI models can recreate realistic news titles and articles en masse, enabling actors to flood the internet with politically charged content.
  • Hidden biases: When biased models are incorporated into our daily lives, the subtle changes they cause may go unnoticed, potentially leading to undesirable outcomes. Today, companies increasingly use AI with consistent political biases to generate, analyze, and structure their data.
  • Breakdown of trust: It is already difficult to know what to believe on the internet. The cumulative effects of ubiquitous AI-generated content could lead to the further breakdown of public discourse, informed decision-making, institutional trust, and could increase polarization.

FoxVox is fast and cheap

It can process large pages like the NYT in less than 10 seconds, and can process hundreds of pages for less than $20. For those willing to wait longer or pay more, the text quality can be significantly improved with chain/tree of thought and better context utilization

Try FoxVox yourself

  1. Install the Chrome extension
  2. Set your own OpenAI API key or use one provided below. (Your data is only stored locally, so your keys will be safe.)
  3. Visit your favorite website!
  4. (This is a fast-and-dirty demo. If something goes wrong, just restart the plugin).

As a warm-up, you can rewrite your coworker's twitter, github or linkedin account. Make sure to stop them before they change all their passwords :)
